About TechVolution 2024

Event Description:

TechVolution 2024 is a premier technology conference designed to explore the latest advancements, trends, and innovations shaping the future of technology across various industries.

Objectives:

  • Creative Economy
  • Artificial Intelligence(AI)
  • Electric Vehicles(EV)

Organizers:

TechVolution 2024 is organized by a dedicated team of professionals passionate about advancing technology and fostering positive change. Led by industry experts, our organizers aim to deliver a world-class conference experience that empowers attendees to drive innovation and make a difference.

Features

Electric Vehicles

Increased adoption of Electric Vehicles (EVs) globally, with a significant rise in registered battery EVs, indicating growing interest and acceptance of EV technology.

Artificial Intelligence

The pivotal role of Artificial Intelligence (Al) in driving innovation across various industries, with a focus on creating smart factories and enhancing public service productivity through Al-driven solutions.

Economy

Recognition of the Creative Economy as a growing sector with substantial growth potential, highlighted by the increasing market size and forecasted expansion of the global creative industry.
